Why This Teaches 3DGS

  1. Gaussians are already fitted. You only change camera and render parameters at inference.
  2. Near/far plane and frustum immediately change visible splats, showing view-dependent culling.
  3. FOV changes projected footprint size, changing apparent density and detail.
  4. Alpha compositing approximates how many translucent Gaussians blend into final pixels.
